# copywriting

How David Ondrej likes his copy written.

## authentic copy

For authentic copy, always write in all lowercase. No capital letters, not even at sentence starts or in "david ondrej".

Not all copy is authentic copy. When unsure whether a piece should be authentic-style or standard, ask the user.

## principles

- high perceived value, never needy or salesy
- a bit of mystery beats overexplaining ("access to david's personal skills")
- super concise. cut every word that doesn't earn its place
- plain english. the simpler the better. the greatest copy reads like fifth grader english
- short, clear sentences
